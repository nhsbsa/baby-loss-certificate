Request a baby loss certificate

Use this service to request a certificate in memory of your baby.

The certificate recognises a baby loss before 24 weeks of pregnancy.

You can find out more about the certificate.

Before you start

You can use this service if you:

  • have had a baby loss before 24 weeks of pregnancy
  • have had a baby loss in the last 5 years in England
  • have a parental relationship to the baby
  • are aged 13 or over
  • live in the UK

You'll need:

  • permission from another parent if you choose to name them on the certificate
  • your postcode
The certificate is not a legal document.

By using this service you are agreeing to its terms of use and privacy policy.
