What happens next

The other parent will have 7 days to give permission and confirm their details.

They may choose not to be named on the certificate. If you would like a certificate with only your name as a parent of your baby, you'll need to start again.

If you've lost more than one pregnancy, you can request another certificate.

Support organisations

The following organisations give support and advice:

  • NHS How to access mental health services
  • Antenatal Results and Choices (ARC)
  • Daddy’s with Angels
  • Petals
  • Sands
  • Saying Goodbye
  • The Ectopic Pregnancy Trust
  • The Miscarriage Association
  • Tommy's
  • Twins Trust
