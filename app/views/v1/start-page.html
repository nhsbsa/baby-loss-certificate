Request a pregnancy loss certificate

You can request a pregnancy loss certificate in memory of your loss.

The certificate recognises a pregnancy loss before 24 weeks.

It is free and will be sent by post.

The certificate will show:

  • name of mother
  • name of second parent (optional)
  • baby’s name (optional)
  • baby’s sex (optional)
  • date of loss
  • place of loss

The certificate is not a legal document.

You can use this service if you:

  • are over [TBC] years old
  • have experienced a pregnancy loss before 24 weeks
  • have experienced a pregnancy loss in the last 5 years in England
  • live in England
  • (have a parental relationship to the pregnancy loss (biological mother, etc)
