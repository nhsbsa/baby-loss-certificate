Request a pregnancy loss certificate

Use this service to request a certificate in memory of your pregnancy loss.

The certificate is not a legal document but recognises a pregnancy loss before 24 weeks.

You can find out more about the certificate.

Before you start

You can use this service if you:

  • are over 13 years old
  • have experienced a pregnancy loss before 24 weeks
  • have experienced a pregnancy loss in the last 5 years in England
  • live in England
  • have a parental relationship to the pregnancy loss

You'll need:

  • to have received permission from any other parents you want to name on the certificate
  • NHS number or postcode registered with your GP
  • access to the mobile phone or email address registered with your GP

By using this service you are agreeing to its terms of use and privacy policy.
